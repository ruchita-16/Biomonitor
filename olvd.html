What is Oxygen Level?

The oxygen level indicates the percentage of oxygen in your blood. A normal oxygen level typically ranges from 95% to 100%.

Why is it Important?

Maintaining a proper oxygen level is crucial for your body's overall health. Low oxygen levels can lead to serious health issues, including hypoxemia.

How to Improve Oxygen Levels?

To improve your oxygen levels, you can practice deep breathing exercises, stay active, and ensure you are in a well-ventilated environment.
